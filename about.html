About Blockbuster

Our story, our vision, and our commitment to excellence.

Our Mission

To create premium yet accessible living spaces that redefine luxury for every family. Our mission is to design homes that embody comfort, connectivity, and class — ensuring that every resident experiences a lifestyle filled with joy, convenience, and pride of ownership.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in Mira-Bhayandar’s real estate landscape by crafting landmark developments that blend modern architecture, smart planning, and people centered design. We envision a community where every home reflects success, serenity, and the true feeling of bliss

Our Commitment

  • Affordable Luxury: We bring premium living within everyone’s reach—without compromising on quality or comfort.
  • Prime Connectivity: Strategically located in Kanakia, Mira Road, Blockbuster ensures you stay connected to every part of Mumbai with ease.
  • Smart Design: Each home is planned for functionality, elegance, and spaciousness—tailored to modern family needs.
  • Quality Assurance: Backed by Bhutra Group’s legacy of trust and excellence, we build with integrity, precision, and long-term reliability.
  • Community Living: Beyond walls and amenities, we build spaces that nurture bonds, joy, and togetherness.
